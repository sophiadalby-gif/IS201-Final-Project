Fun Holiday Traditions

  • Make homemade hot chocolate and add unique toppings like crushed candy canes or cinnamon sticks.
  • Drive around to look at Christmas lights while listening to your favorite holiday playlist.
  • Have a Christmas pajama night and watch nostalgic holiday movies.
  • Decorate sugar cookies and deliver them to neighbors or friends.
  • Write a letter to your future self to open next Christmas.
  • Read the Nativity story together on Christmas Eve.
